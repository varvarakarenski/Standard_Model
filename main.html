The Standard Model of Particle Physics

The Standard Model of Particle Physics dictates information about the universe's elementary particles. Elementary particles are the simplest form of particles that make up eveything else. Essentially, it serves as a 'cheat sheet' or periodic table for elementary particles. The Standard Model depicts a particle's symbol, mass, charge, and spin.
